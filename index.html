🌿 Ganja: Truth Behind the Smoke 🌿

What is Ganja?

Ganja, also known as cannabis or marijuana, is a psychoactive plant primarily used for recreational or medicinal purposes. The active chemical compound, THC (Tetrahydrocannabinol), affects the brain, causing mood changes, altered senses, and a feeling of being “high”.

How Ganja is Produced?

Ganja is produced by cultivating the cannabis plant. The process includes:

  • 🌱 Growing cannabis plants in controlled environments
  • 🌞 Providing adequate light, water, and nutrients
  • 🌸 Harvesting buds when they mature
  • 🌬️ Drying and curing the flowers
  • 📦 Packaging for distribution

Negative Impacts of Ganja

  • 🧠 Memory loss and cognitive decline
  • 💤 Laziness, demotivation, and sleep disorders
  • 🫀 Heart and lung issues (when smoked)
  • ⚖️ Legal problems and addiction risk
  • 😔 Social withdrawal and poor academic/work performance

Who is a Ganjakhor?

A Ganjakhor is a slang term used for someone who is addicted to consuming ganja. They often rely on marijuana to feel relaxed, escape reality, or manage emotions.

Behavior of a Ganjakhor

  • 😵 Frequent zoning out or loss of attention
  • 🍟 Craving for snacks (“munchies”)
  • 📉 Decline in productivity and motivation
  • 😶 Social isolation or strange speech
  • 😴 Irregular sleeping patterns
  • 😬 Paranoia or sudden mood swings
